Title: Schindler’s List (1993)
IMDb Rating: 9.0

Summary:
Set during World War II, Schindler’s List follows the story of Oskar Schindler, a German businessman who saves the lives of over a thousand Jewish refugees. 
Schindler initially exploits the war for financial gain, employing Jews in his factory as cheap labor. However, as the horrors of the Holocaust unfold, 
he transforms into a compassionate savior, risking his life to protect his Jewish workers.

Directed by Steven Spielberg, the film is a haunting yet powerful portrayal of the human capacity for both evil and good. 
Its black-and-white cinematography and emotional depth make it a cinematic masterpiece.
