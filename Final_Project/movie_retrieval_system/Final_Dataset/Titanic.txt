Title: Titanic (1997)
IMDb Rating: 7.9

Summary:
Titanic tells the tragic love story of Jack and Rose, who come from different social classes but find themselves drawn to each other aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic. 
Their romance blossoms despite the pressures of society, but disaster strikes when the ship hits an iceberg, leading to one of the deadliest maritime disasters in history.

Directed by James Cameron, Titanic is both a love story and a historical drama, showcasing breathtaking visuals and a powerful narrative. 
The film became a global sensation, winning multiple Academy Awards and leaving a lasting cultural impact.
