Title: The Silence of the Lambs (1991)
IMDb Rating: 8.6

Summary:
The Silence of the Lambs follows FBI trainee Clarice Starling as she seeks the help of imprisoned cannibalistic serial killer Dr. Hannibal Lecter to catch another killer, Buffalo Bill. 
As Clarice engages in psychological games with Lecter, she uncovers chilling truths about herself and the world of serial murderers.

Directed by Jonathan Demme, the film is a tense thriller that explores the darker aspects of human psychology. 
It is hailed for its gripping performances, particularly by Anthony Hopkins and Jodie Foster, and its masterful suspense.
