Title: Parasite (2019)
IMDb Rating: 8.5

Summary:
Parasite tells the story of two families from drastically different socio-economic backgrounds. 
The impoverished Kim family cleverly infiltrates the lives of the wealthy Park family, securing various jobs in their household. 
As the Kims grow closer to the Parks, they uncover hidden secrets within the mansion, leading to a series of unexpected events that escalate tensions between the two families.

The film masterfully blends elements of drama, suspense, and dark humor, offering sharp social commentary on class inequality. 
Director Bong Joon-ho explores how desperation can lead to moral corruption, making Parasite a gripping and thought-provoking film.
