Title: La La Land (2016)
IMDb Rating: 8.0

Summary:
La La Land is a modern-day musical that follows the love story of Mia, an aspiring actress, and Sebastian, a jazz musician, as they pursue their dreams in Los Angeles. 
The film explores the highs and lows of their relationship, balancing the tension between love and ambition.

Directed by Damien Chazelle, La La Land is a visually stunning homage to classic Hollywood musicals. 
Its catchy songs, dazzling choreography, and heartfelt performances have made it a beloved film, earning critical acclaim and numerous accolades.
