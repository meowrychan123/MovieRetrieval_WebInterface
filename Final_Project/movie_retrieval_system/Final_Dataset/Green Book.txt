Title: Green Book (2018)
IMDb Rating: 8.2

Summary:
Green Book is a heartwarming drama about the unlikely friendship between Tony Lip, a working-class Italian-American bouncer, and Dr. Don Shirley, an African-American classical pianist. 
Set during the 1960s, the two men embark on a concert tour through the racially segregated southern United States, confronting prejudice and social barriers along the way.

Directed by Peter Farrelly, Green Book is a poignant exploration of race, class, and friendship. 
Its blend of humor and emotional depth made it a critical success, earning the Academy Award for Best Picture.
