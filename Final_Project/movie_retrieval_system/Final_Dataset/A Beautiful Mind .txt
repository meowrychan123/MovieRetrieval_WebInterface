Title: A Beautiful Mind (2001)
IMDb Rating: 8.2

Summary:
A Beautiful Mind tells the life story of John Nash, a brilliant mathematician who struggles with paranoid schizophrenia. 
The film follows Nash's journey from the heights of academic success to his battles with mental illness, eventually leading to his Nobel Prize-winning achievements.

Directed by Ron Howard, the film blends biography, drama, and romance, presenting a compelling portrait of both genius and mental struggle. 
Russell Crowe's performance as Nash is celebrated for its emotional depth, making A Beautiful Mind a touching and inspiring film.
