Title: Braveheart (1995)
IMDb Rating: 8.4

Summary:
Braveheart tells the story of William Wallace, a Scottish warrior who leads his countrymen in a rebellion against English tyranny in the 13th century. 
Driven by the brutal execution of his wife, Wallace fights for Scottish independence with unwavering determination, becoming a legendary figure in history.

Directed by and starring Mel Gibson, Braveheart is an epic tale of love, freedom, and sacrifice. 
The film is known for its thrilling battle scenes and emotional depth, capturing the spirit of Scotland's fight for freedom.
