Title: The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King (2003)
IMDb Rating: 9.0

Summary:
The Return of the King is the epic conclusion to The Lord of the Rings trilogy, following the final struggle for control over Middle-earth. 
As Frodo and Sam draw closer to Mount Doom to destroy the One Ring, Aragorn rises to claim his rightful throne as the king of Gondor. 
The armies of good and evil face off in the ultimate battle, with the fate of the world hanging in the balance.

Directed by Peter Jackson, the film is a visual and emotional triumph, seamlessly blending action, drama, and fantasy. 
Its sweeping scope and poignant conclusion earned it 11 Academy Awards, cementing its legacy.
