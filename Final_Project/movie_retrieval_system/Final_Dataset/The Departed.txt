Title: The Departed (2006)
IMDb Rating: 8.5

Summary:
The Departed is a crime thriller about an undercover cop, Billy Costigan, who infiltrates the Irish mob in Boston, and a mole, Colin Sullivan, who has infiltrated the police force for the mob. 
As both men attempt to uncover each other's identities, the tension escalates into a deadly game of cat and mouse.

Directed by Martin Scorsese, the film is a gritty exploration of loyalty, betrayal, and identity. 
Its complex narrative and outstanding performances make it one of Scorsese's finest works, earning it several Academy Awards.
