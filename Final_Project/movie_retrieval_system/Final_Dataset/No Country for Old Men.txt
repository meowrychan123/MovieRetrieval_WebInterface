Title: No Country for Old Men (2007)
IMDb Rating: 8.2

Summary:
No Country for Old Men is a tense thriller that follows the violent aftermath of a drug deal gone wrong in rural Texas. 
Llewelyn Moss stumbles upon a briefcase full of money at the crime scene, setting off a deadly chase involving a psychopathic hitman, Anton Chigurh, and a weary sheriff. 
Chigurh’s relentless pursuit of Moss makes for a suspenseful, nail-biting cat-and-mouse game.

The film, directed by the Coen brothers, explores themes of fate, morality, and the changing landscape of justice. 
Its chilling atmosphere and Javier Bardem’s menacing performance as Chigurh have made it a modern classic.
