Title: Gladiator (2000)
IMDb Rating: 8.5

Summary:
Gladiator follows Maximus Decimus Meridius, a Roman general who is betrayed by the corrupt Commodus, the son of the emperor. 
After being forced into slavery and becoming a gladiator, Maximus rises through the ranks of the Colosseum, 
seeking revenge for the murder of his family and the betrayal of his army. His journey is one of honor, vengeance, and redemption.

Ridley Scott's Gladiator is known for its epic battle scenes, stunning visuals, and powerful performances. 
The film reflects on themes of loyalty, power, and justice, earning its place as one of the best historical dramas.
