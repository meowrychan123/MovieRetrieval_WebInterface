Title: The King's Speech (2010)
IMDb Rating: 8.0

Summary:
The King's Speech tells the true story of King George VI's struggle to overcome a debilitating speech impediment as he ascends the British throne. 
With the help of an unorthodox speech therapist, Lionel Logue, George VI finds his voice and leads the country during a time of crisis.

Directed by Tom Hooper, the film is an inspiring drama that explores themes of leadership, courage, and perseverance. 
Colin Firth's portrayal of the king earned him an Academy Award, and the film's emotional depth and historical significance resonate with audiences.
