Title: Birdman (2014)
IMDb Rating: 7.7

Summary:
Birdman tells the story of Riggan Thomson, a washed-up actor best known for playing a superhero in the past. 
Determined to be taken seriously as an artist, Riggan stages a Broadway play to revive his career. 
As opening night approaches, Riggan’s personal and professional struggles intensify, blurring the line between reality and his past superhero persona.

The film, directed by Alejandro G. Iñárritu, is renowned for its innovative single-take style and themes of self-worth, artistic integrity, and mental health. 
Birdman is both a satire of the entertainment industry and a poignant look at personal redemption.
