Title: Argo (2012)
IMDb Rating: 7.7

Summary:
Argo is based on the true story of a daring CIA operation to rescue six American diplomats from Tehran during the 1979 Iranian hostage crisis. 
The mission, led by CIA agent Tony Mendez, involves creating a fake science fiction film as a cover to smuggle the hostages out of Iran.

Directed by and starring Ben Affleck, Argo is a tense political thriller that balances suspense with moments of dark humor. 
The film's gripping narrative and historical significance earned it critical acclaim and the Academy Award for Best Picture.
