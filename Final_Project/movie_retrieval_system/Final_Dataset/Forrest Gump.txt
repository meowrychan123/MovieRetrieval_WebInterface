Title: Forrest Gump (1994)
IMDb Rating: 8.8

Summary:
Forrest Gump tells the life story of a simple yet kind-hearted man from Alabama who unwittingly becomes involved in many significant historical events of the 20th century. 
From meeting Presidents to inspiring a famous smiley face logo, Forrest’s unintentional impact on the world is both heartwarming and humorous. 
Despite his low IQ, his love for his childhood friend Jenny is unwavering, and it drives much of his journey.

The film’s charm lies in its balance of personal and historical narratives, exploring themes of destiny, love, and perseverance. 
Tom Hanks' iconic portrayal of Forrest makes this a deeply touching and memorable film.
