Title: Slumdog Millionaire (2008)
IMDb Rating: 8.0

Summary:
Slumdog Millionaire tells the rags-to-riches story of Jamal Malik, a young man from the slums of Mumbai who becomes a contestant on the Indian version of 'Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?' 
Through flashbacks, the film reveals how Jamal's life experiences have provided him with the knowledge to answer the quiz questions, ultimately leading to his success.

Directed by Danny Boyle, the film is a vibrant mix of drama, romance, and social commentary. 
It highlights the resilience of the human spirit and the power of destiny, earning critical acclaim and multiple Academy Awards.
